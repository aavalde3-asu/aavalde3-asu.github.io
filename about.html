Bio:


My name is Antonio Valdez, and I am a photography student at Arizona State University.

Artist Statement

Born and raised in the dry heat and concrete of Phoenix, Arizona, I spent all my life soaking in the world around me, and it was not until recently that I felt the need to capture what I see and envision, and to show this to others.
My main focus is to express how I personally perceive the overwhelming combination of life: people, nature, design, land, history, memory, life, and death.
